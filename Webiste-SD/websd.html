About Me

Hi my name is, Sharon Dsouza.

I'm from Mumbai, India. Currently a Computer Science graduate, from St. Francis Institue of Technology under the University of Mumbai. My areas of academic interests range over Data Sciences and Web Designing. My proficiency in technical languages is Python, Java, JavaScript, CSS, HTML, PHP, Git. I've worked with frameworks such as SpringBoot, Flask, and Bootstrap4. I'm always looking for new concepts to learn and new ways to improve. During the corse of my academics, apart from the classroom I was part of many cultural committees that helped me develop a number of soft skills. In my second year I was part of my IEEE Student Branch workforce, in my Third Year I was given the responsibilty to lead my IEEE student branch in the capacity of a General Secretary which helped me coordinate tasks between various sub-committees as well as intra-committees. It gave me the opportunity to conduct workshops in order to make relevant industry skills known as well as taught to students, not only in my college but students from colleges all over Mumbai were able to attend. I was also part of the Women in IEEE committee and conducted the Orientation Program for the Freshers. I was part of the Marketing committee for my college's culural fest Iris. This helped me develop interpersonal skills and gave me the confidence to be more vocal about my opinions as well as work with a team made up of my peers.

In my Third Year of Engineering, I interned at McSun Design Studio as a Software Intern for a period of four months. During the course of the internship my responsibilities included a full revamp of the company website. I Redesigned the layout to reflect a more modern aesthetic with a new homepage layout, added animations, social media links and icons, “INTERIORS” category section. Also, maintained functionality and fixed reported errors on the (interior design) website using PHP, HTML and CSS. Being a part of this company taught me what being a part of a team really meant. Even though I was one of the youngest employees, I always had a platform to share my ideas and have discussions as an equally valuable member of the team.
